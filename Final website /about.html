About Us

Rooting for Trees was established in 2010. The intent of this organization is two-fold :

  1. Educate the world regarding the great damages that our ecosystem is suffering due to uncontrolled and unmitigated felling of trees across the Amazon heartland, India, and Australia â€“ specific regions that were naturally gifted with the green cover that our earth used to have in abundance and
  2. Work with charitable organizations and NGOs that have been doing a phenomenal job in controlling damage and fighting for the rights of our ecosystem and bring like-minded people and companies together for planting trees.

This way we root for the old world that we were gifted with to manage, grow, and hand over to the next generation in one piece. It is a long way to go but a start itself is a good start.
